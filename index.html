Candidate for Norristown Area School District Board of School Directors

About

John graduated Norristown Area High School in 2016; only last year! Currently John is in his first year of college studying Political Science at Temple University. John also currently serves as a Democratic committee-person in West Norrition Township. John and his family are invested in Norristown, John received 13 years of a education from the Norristown Area School Distict, as did his sister. Their mother has worked in the district for 14 years.  John has a close relationship to Norristown

John is only 19 years old, which is a big reason as to why he is running. John has realized that the only way for the Democratic party and progressive ideas to advance, is by embracing younger people into the party. John believes he can bring a new and fresh voice. John is not the voice of one, but many.

Having only graduated from Norristown Area High School last year, John has a perspective of the district that the Board has ever heard before. John understands how to improve student lives and take this district in a modern direction. Students, staff, and the faculty could appreciate a voice that understands the district from firsthand experiences.

I ask you to please support my campaign as we aim to get younger people involved in the political process and bring a fresh voice to the board!
